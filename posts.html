Manali Essentials

Best time: Oct–Feb for snow, Mar–Jun for pleasant weather. Stay near Old Manali for cafés and riverside walks.

Goa in 3 Days

Day 1: Baga & Calangute • Day 2: Anjuna & Vagator • Day 3: Old Goa & Dona Paula. Rent a scooter and start early.

Jaipur Forts

Amber Fort, Nahargarh at sunset, Hawa Mahal early morning for photos. Try local kachori and lassi.
